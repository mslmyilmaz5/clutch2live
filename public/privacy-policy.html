Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Clutch2Live ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile games.

1. Data We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or camera/microphone data through our games.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Our games use Google AdMob as a third-party advertising service. AdMob may collect certain information automatically to provide, personalize, and measure ads. This may include:

  • Device information (model, OS version, language, network type)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID / IDFA where applicable)
  • Approximate location inferred from IP address
  • Ad interaction and performance data

Data collected by AdMob is processed under Google's own privacy terms. Please review Google's policy for more details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Permissions

Our games do not request camera, microphone, contacts, or similar sensitive permissions for gameplay.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Limited data may be shared with our advertising provider (AdMob) only for ad delivery and analytics purposes.

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers for these games. Any retention related to advertising data is managed by third-party providers under their own policies.

7. Your Choices

You can manage ad personalization and identifiers through your device privacy settings (for example, resetting or limiting ad tracking where available).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: hello@clutch2live.com
